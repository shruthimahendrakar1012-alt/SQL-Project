# SQL-Project
Organizations often struggle with managing large amounts of employee-related information such as personal details, department assignments, job roles, salary records, qualifications, and leave data. Maintaining this information manually or across disconnected systems can lead to data duplication, errors, difficulty in generating reports, and inefficient HR decision-making.
 The organization requires a centralized database system that can store, manage, and analyze employee information efficiently. A structured Employee Management System is needed to improve data accuracy, automate HR reporting, monitor payroll expenses, track employee skills, and support better workforce planning.

"To design and develop a centralized SQL-based Employee Management System that efficiently stores, manages, and analyzes employee, payroll, qualification, and leave data while providing accurate insights for effective HR management and decision-making."
